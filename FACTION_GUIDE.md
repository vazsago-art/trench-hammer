## Faction Mechanics

### Selecting a Faction

1. Open the Army Builder
2. Use the **Faction Selector** dropdown
3. Choose from all 33 available factions
4. The UI updates to show available units for that faction

### Building Your Warband

Each faction provides:
- **Unit options** - Available warriors to recruit
- **Point costs** - Credit costs for each unit
- **Equipment** - Wargear selection
- **Keywords** - Faction identity and ability triggers
- **Stats** - Movement, skill, and armor values

### Warband Requirements

- **Point limit** - Total cost cannot exceed warband point value
- **Unit count** - Mix and match units as desired
- **Minimum starting models** - Each faction has different minimums
- **Special rules** - Some units have unique abilities

---

## Getting Started

1. **Select a faction** that appeals to you
2. **Review available units** for that faction
3. **Add units to your warband** within point limits
4. **Customize equipment** for each unit
5. **Export your army list** for printing or sharing

---

## Tips for New Players

- **Start with Imperial factions** for a balanced experience
- **Chaos factions** offer more specialized abilities
- **Xenos factions** have unique mechanics and strengths
- **Outlaw factions** work best as smaller, scrappy bands
- **Mix unit types** for balanced forces
