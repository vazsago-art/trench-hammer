### Chaos Daemons

**Faction Type:** Multi-Legion System (Khorne, Nurgle, Slaanesh, Tzeentch, Undivided)

**Special Rules:**
- Daemon Legions: Can recruit from any of the four legions with keyword restrictions
- Warp Entities: Models can have DEEP STRIKE keyword
- The Great Game: Track god ascendency with benefits/penalties/rewards

**Elites:**
- **Daemon Prince** (210 Credits, or 190 for Prince of Darkness) - 8" movement, +2 Ranged, +2 Melee, 60mm base
  - Variants: Prince of Darkness (-20), Prince of Khorne, Prince of Nurgle, Prince of Slaanesh, Prince of Tzeentch
  - Abilities: Daemonic Resistance, Overlord
  - Keywords: DAEMON, ELITE, LARGE, LEADER, STRONG, TOUGH

- **Bloodmaster** (70 Credits) - 8" movement, +0 Ranged, +2 Melee, 60mm base
  - Abilities: Daemonic Resistance, A Gory Path, Master of Blood
  - Keywords: DAEMON, ELITE, KHORNE, LEADER, TOUGH

- **Skullmaster** (90 Credits) - 8" movement, +0 Ranged, +2 Melee, 60mm base
  - Abilities: Daemonic Resistance
  - Keywords: DAEMON, ELITE, LARGE, KHORNE, TOUGH

- **Infernal Enrapturess** (115 Credits) - 8" movement, +2 Ranged, +2 Melee, 60x35mm base
  - Abilities: Daemonic Resistance, Discordant Disruption, Harmonic Alignment

- **Tranceweaver** (70 Credits + powers cost) - 8" movement, +0 Ranged, +2 Melee, 25mm base
  - Can purchase up to 3 powers from Soulstain Psychic Discipline
  - Keywords: DAEMON, ELITE, PSYKER 1, SLAANESH

- **Poxbringer** (70 Credits + powers cost) - 6" movement, +1 Ranged, +2 Melee, 32mm base

**Faction Type:** Chaos Space Marines with variants (Death Guard, Emperor's Children, Thousand Sons, World Eaters)

**Special Rules:**
- Dark Pacts: Models can attempt Dark Pacts for +1 DICE to Hit or +1 INJURY DICE (gain BLOOD MARKER)
- Dread Reputation: Track Personal, Dark Gods, and Warfleet Reputation (1-8 range)
- Reputation ranges: 7-8 Favored, 1-2 Forsaken with specific benefits/penalties

**Patrons:** Chaos Undivided, Chaos Warlord, or Heretek Lord

**Notable Elites/Units:**
- **Chaos Terminator** - Wearing Terminator Armour
- **Raptor** - Equipped with Jump Pack
- **Chaos Space Marine** - Basic elite infantry

**Marks of Chaos Available:**
- Mark of Darkness (10 credits) - Ranged attacks -1 DICE to Hit
- Mark of Khorne (15 credits) - +1 INJURY MODIFIER melee, KHORNE Keyword
- Mark of Nurgle (15 credits)
- Mark of Slaanesh (15 credits)  
- Mark of Tzeentch (15 credits)
- Limit: 1 per model, max 2 of each mark in warband

## GROUP 2: IMPERIAL FACTIONS

### Adeptus Astartes

**Faction Type:** Space Marines with Chapter variants (Ultramarines, Black Templars, Blood Angels, Dark Angels, Deathwatch, Grey Knights, Imperial Fists, Iron Hands, Legion of the Damned, Ravenguard, Salamanders, Space Wolves, White Scars)

**Special Rules:**
- And They Shall Know No Fear: ASTARTES models have NEGATE FEAR
- The Rubicon Primaris: Non-Dreadnought ASTARTES can upgrade to Primaris (+5 credits)
- Gene Seeds: Recover Gene Seeds when models become casualties (30 credit discount on new recruit)

**Elites Available:** 6 models maximum
- Captain (cost varies by chapter selection)
- Librarian
- Apothecary
- Techmarine
- Dreadnought
- Terminator Squad Leader
- Assault Marine
- Vanguard Veteran
- Scout Marine

**Troops:** 
- Tactical Marine
- Assault Marine  
- Scout Marine
- Terminator
- Bladeguard Veteran

**Patrons:** Chapter Master or The Indomitus Crusade
- Chapter-specific patrons with specialized abilities for each chapter variant

**Keywords:** ASTARTES, SPACE MARINE, [CHAPTER] (e.g., ULTRAMARINES, BLOOD ANGELS)

### Astra Militarum

**Faction Type:** Imperial Guard with special command system

**Special Rules:**
- Voice of Command: Elite models can issue Orders to non-Elite MILITARUM within 6"
- Commendations: Campaign-based achievement system for model advancement
- 12 different commendation types available

**Elites:**
- **Castellan** (70 Credits) - 6" movement, +2 Ranged, +2 Melee, 25-28mm base
  - Abilities: Bring it Down!, Get Back in the Fight!
  - Keywords: ELITE, LEADER, MILITARUM, TOUGH
  - Requirement: 1 in warband (required leader)

- **Commissar** (40 Credits + pistol cost) - 6" movement, +1 Ranged, +1 Melee, 25-28mm base
  - Ability: Summary Execution
  - Keywords: ELITE, MILITARUM
  - Allowed: 0-2

- **Primaris Psyker** (40 Credits + weapon + powers) - 6" movement, +1 Ranged, +0 Melee, 25-28mm base
  - Can purchase up to 3 powers from Psykana Discipline
  - Keywords: ELITE, MILITARUM, PSYKER 1
  - Allowed: 0-1

**Troops:**
- **Conscript** (18 Credits + 7 for weapons) - 6" movement, +0 Ranged, -1 Melee, 25mm base
  - Can upgrade to Guardsman after 2+ missions, Glorious Deed, or taking Elite Out of Action
  - Keywords: MILITARUM, NO PROMOTION

- **Guardsman** (30 Credits) - 6" movement, +0 Ranged, +0 Melee, 25mm base
  - Standard infantry, fully customizable
  - Keywords: MILITARUM

**Patrons:** Lord General Militant, Administratum Prefectus, Indomitus Crusade, or Lord Inquisitor

**Orders Available:**
1. Duty and Honour - +1 INJURY DICE melee attacks
2. First Rank, Fire! Second Rank, Fire! - Reduced BLOOD MARKERS for Bloodbath
3. Fix Bayonets! - Free melee attack when charged
4. Move! Move! Move! - +2" movement, +1 DICE to movement rolls
5. Take Aim! - +1 DICE to Hit ranged, ignore Long Range
6. Take Cover! - -1 or -2 flat injury modifier

- Black Market Acquisitions: Purchase from other faction shops

- Emperor's Judgement: Once per battle direct damage

- Fleetmaster: Exploration skills

### The Inquisition

**Faction Type:** Inquisitors with Chamber Militant system

**Special Rules:**
- Ordo: Choose one of three Ordos (Xenos, Hereticus, Malleus)
- Requisitions: Start with 11 Glory, 500 credits, -200 credit threshold
- Shadow Operations: Random mission objectives each battle (D6 roll)

**Elites:**
- **Inquisitor** (55 Credits + 15 for Rosarius) - Required leader
  - Multiple upgrades available (Duelist, Frightening Reputation, Great Strength, Pistoleer, Psyker)
- **Interrogator** (50 Credits) - Support elite
- **Acolyte** options vary by Ordo

**Troops:** Varies by chosen Ordo

**Patrons:** Lord Inquisitor Shared Patron (only option)

**Ordos Available:**
1. **Ordo Xenos** - Against alien threats
2. **Ordo Hereticus** - Against heretics and chaos
3. **Ordo Malleus** - Against daemons

**Shadow Operations (D6):**
1. Agent Provocateur - Eliminate chosen enemy elite
2. Ancient Artefact - Recover central artifact
3. Diabolical Ritual - Neutralize ritual sites
4. Informant - Contact informant
5. Sympathiser - Models in enemy deployment zone
6. Threat of Uprising - More models in own deployment zone
